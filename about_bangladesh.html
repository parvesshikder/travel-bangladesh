About Bangladesh

Bangaldesh is one of the developing coutries located in South Asia sharing its borders with India and Myanmar. Bangladesh is one of the few countries located in South Asia where there is natural essence present in both urban and rural life. It can be also be said that people of Bangladesh are very humble and hardworking.

Bangladesh has a very rich historal backgorund comapred to its neighbouring countries from the Mughal Empire to British they all once ruled this land. The last war which Bangladesh won was against Pakistan .Before 1971 bangladesh was a part of Pakistan but after 9 months of war Bangladesh achieved victory through alot of sacrifice but after the war the country had to suffer alot and still it's developing. Bangladesh is the only country in the world that has fought for his motherlanguage which is Bangla. Bangladesh is one of the over populated country in the world compared to its size. As a result some population of people live under the poverty line.
